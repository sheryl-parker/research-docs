# HESA Data Futures Schema Comparison Report (2024 vs 2025)

## 1. Executive Summary

This report provides a full analysis of the differences between the 2024 and 2025 HESA Data Futures schemas (24056_1_2_0) and (25056_1_0_0). It examines structural, argument and validation-level changes, and assesses their impacts on the university data systems and workflows (SITS, Alteryx, XML generation, and validation) for the 2025 return cycle.

---

## 2. Context and Objectives

THE HESA Data Futures schemas are released yearly to align with regulatory changes, and reflect new policy or funding reporting requirements. This review identifies changes from 2024 to 2025 and their potential implications.
